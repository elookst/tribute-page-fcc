Nelson Mandela

Hero of South Africa

Nelson Mandela, second from right, walking with the people of South Africa in celebration.

Here's a timeline of the life of Nelson Mandela:

  • July 18, 1918: Nelson Mandela is born in the village of Mvezo, in Umtata, then part of South Africa's Cape Province.
  • 1939: Mandela attended University of Fort Hare in Alice, Eastern Cape to complete a Bachelor of Arts; however, Mandela was later suspended from the school for being involved in a boycott against the quality of the food & did not return to complete his degree.
